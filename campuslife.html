Extra Curricular Activities

NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. NSS is aimed at developing students' personality through community service.

NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces. It provides opportunities for students to take part in military activities and learn about the defense forces.

Sports

Our campus offers excellent sports facilities, encouraging students to engage in physical activities. Sports like cricket, football, basketball, and athletics are promoted with regular training sessions.

Cultural Activities

The cultural activities provide students with a platform to showcase their talents in music, dance, drama, and fine arts. Annual cultural festivals and events are organized to celebrate diversity.

Drama Club

The Drama Club encourages students to explore their theatrical talents. Students can participate in plays, skits, and drama workshops, enhancing their acting and production skills.

Photography Club

The Photography Club encourages students to explore their photography skills. Students can participate in different workshops, enhancing their photography skills.
